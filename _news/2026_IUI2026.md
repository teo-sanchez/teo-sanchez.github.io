🥳 Our paper, *Mental Models in Human-AI Interaction: Systematic Review of Empirical Methodologies and Guidelines*, co-authored with Oleksandra Vereschak and Ophelia Deroy, has been accepted to [IUI 2026](https://iui.acm.org/2026/), which will take place in Paphos, Cyprus, on March 23–26. The preprint is [now publicly available](https://zenodo.org/records/18663146). This marks an important milestone for the HAMLU project and reflects a highly productive collaboration!
